A Letter to Life

with whom I am so in love, I fear she might notice me.

Photography Exhibition by Aleksander Allen

Capturing life's beauty in art demands we put ourselves consciously into the passenger seat, sacrificing agency and freedom to live.

Yet this is precisely how we learn the beauty of life.
